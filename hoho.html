03 / Vertical Tampa CDC

Vertical Tampa CDC

Mission Statement

We strengthen communities and improve quality of life by advancing affordable housing, economic mobility, workforce development, educational opportunity, community revitalization, and strategic partnerships.

Vertical Tampa CDC is a mission-driven, faith-based nonprofit created to help families move from housing insecurity toward durable opportunity. Our work begins with real needs in the community, then connects strategy, development partners, and local trust into plans that can actually be built.

Rooted in Tampa Bay and designed for broader impact, we focus on affordable housing, economic mobility, workforce pathways, and neighborhood revitalization. We are not chasing a concept. We are building a repeatable model for stronger communities.

What we make possible Stable homes, stronger families, and development that serves people first.

Every initiative is shaped around execution: clear partners, responsible growth, and outcomes that can be measured in families supported and neighborhoods strengthened.

01 Mission-led development We align faith-based service with practical development strategy so community care can become real housing capacity.
02 Tampa Bay roots Our foundation is local, but the model is built to scale through trusted partnerships and disciplined execution.
03 People-first outcomes We measure progress through stable homes, supported families, stronger workforce pathways, and neighborhoods prepared for long-term growth.
04 / The Problem

Across the United States, communities are facing a growing housing crisis

The crisis continues to impact working families, essential workers, and underserved populations, creating ripple effects in economic stability, education, workforce participation, and quality of life.

01

Housing Affordability Crisis

Working families nationwide are struggling to find housing that aligns with their income.

02

Rising Costs vs. Stagnant Wages

Housing prices and rental rates continue to increase faster than income growth.

03

Limited Attainable Housing Supply

There is a significant shortage of housing designed for everyday working individuals and families.

Our Response Mission only matters when it becomes buildable.

Vertical Tampa CDC is structured to move from community need to financing, compliance, design, construction, and repeatable housing models partners can help deliver.
