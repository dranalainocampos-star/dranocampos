Who We Are

Vertical Tampa CDC

Vertical Tampa CDC is a mission-driven, faith-based nonprofit created to help families move from housing insecurity toward durable opportunity.

Rooted in Tampa Bay and designed for broader impact, we focus on affordable housing, economic mobility, workforce pathways, and neighborhood revitalization. We are not chasing a concept. We are building a repeatable model for stronger communities.

Mission-led development aligned with practical housing strategy
Tampa Bay foundation designed for replication and trusted partnerships

The Problem

Across the United States, communities are facing a growing housing crisis

The crisis continues to impact working families, essential workers, and underserved populations, creating ripple effects in economic stability, education, workforce participation, and quality of life.

Housing Affordability Crisis

Working families nationwide are struggling to find housing that aligns with their income.

Rising Costs vs. Stagnant Wages

Housing prices and rental rates continue to increase faster than income growth.

Limited Attainable Housing Supply

There is a significant shortage of housing designed for everyday working individuals and families.

Mission only matters when it becomes buildable. Vertical Tampa CDC is structured to move from community need to financing, compliance, design, construction, and repeatable housing models partners can help deliver.
